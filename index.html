WELCOME TO THE COSMIC GARAGE

A garage in a house. The house in a garage.

In my garage there is a bit of everything. My old books, my old games. My bycicle.

Things that I collect and then forget. My skis. Broken things. Random things.

My camping stuff. Cardboard boxes. Plastic boxes. Funny stuff. Empty boxes.

The Christmas tree. Boring old things. Tools. A box that contains the tools.

Stuff.

The garage is the home of all the things that I don't need.

I use them when I have a new idea.

I use them when I leave for a new adventure.

THEY ARE COSMIC

It is not tidy. It is not well-organized.

But it is harmonius.

Things are forming constellations.

Constellations are forming a universe.
